Omelette

An omelet is a good choice for breakfast. It is useful with a large amount of protein and gives energy for the whole coming day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Break eggs
  2. Add milk and salt
  3. Beat eggs and milk
  4. Fry for 2 minutes on each side in a frying pan with oil
